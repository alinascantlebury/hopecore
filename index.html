i enjoy laughing and being optimistic. more people should laugh and be optimistic. my digital zine will explore the idea of hopecore—a practice of intentional optimism in an age where nihilism feels easier than joy. ‘Hopecore’ (and the concept of ‘Finding Feeling’). Like picking wild berries, an act that reconnects us to the earth and reminds us we're part of something larger, this project invites visitors to rediscover forgotten feelings and simple pleasures. In a time when college students and professors alike struggle with hopelessness, hopecore offers a counter-narrative: that laughter, optimism, and our connection to nature aren't naive, but necessary. a reminder that we can choose to find feeling, foster connection, and cultivate hope, even when the world suggests otherwise. this is my call to action: more people should laugh, more people should be optimistic, and more people should remember that we are one with the earth together.
